I used custom CFrame matrix to swap X and Y in the camera output, this creates 3 more camera and wraps the Viewport Frame hardcoded-ly cuz I can't find good solution to make the distortion continuous.

how I "infinite scrolled" (tiled and wrap) it:
once any X or Y position is more than 1(SCALAR POSITION, NOT PIXEL OFFSET), decrease both X and Y by -2 to reposition it back at the start(individually) without losing potential offset that might have exceeded 1,1 keeping the smoothness
if you simply set the position back to "start", some might go off the track cuz you hard reset the position and dismissed the already exceeded position that would've attached the kaleidoscope together.
Also if you used simple tiled %(mod) then I guess it might just offset the entire tile, the distortion will seem swapped when it gotta wrap (lowk that sounded good tho)

local scaleY = 1 - ((row % 2) * 2)
